Projects

Stem cell detection in digital images.

A post image processing projects on digital images of stem cell.The goal was to make an effective algorithm to segment the cells from hazy digital images and track the path of the cells in a maze. The segmentation was done using morphological functions. The data was further used for study of decision making capabilities of the cells using machine learning.

Cox method of Watermarking for images

The method in Cox et al [1997], the watermark insertion and extraction is done in the transform (DCT) domain. Study and comparison of effects on watermark from different types of attacks; resize, removal of part of some image, salt and paper noise and etc.

Designed an algorithm of an N-bit multiplication using 4by2 AMM

Multiplications are commonly followed by additions. In such cases, implementing a multiply-add unit is used to be cost effective. This method was designed to reduce the delay by 20-30% of over all multiplier; this algorithm reduces the additional delay introduced by adder at the end of the multiplication.

Project on VWAP and TWAP-Trading Algorithms on ALTERAs FPGA Board.

Volume Weighted Average Price and Time Weighted Average Price. Designed an efficient algorithm and implemented on Alteras FPGA board. Mainly used by trading firms and banking sector, it was possible to implement low latency calculations on FPGA.
